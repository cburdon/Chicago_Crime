    Chicago Crime Data Summary

    Team Week of the Wildebeests pulled multiple data sets from the City of Chicago to analyze the rate of criminal incidents that occur.

    The initial database for Chicago Crime included 6.5 million rows of the span of 10 years, 2008 to 2018.

    From these datasets we analyzed a variety of factors such as:
    Number of Incidents by Month
    Number of Incidents by Hour
    Number of Incidents by Category
    Number of Incidents by Year


    *The uploaded database only has 853,414 rows due to limitation and time issues.
